ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

Artificial intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems. Specific applications of AI include expert systems, natural language processing, speech recognition and machine vision.

Reactive Memory

It merely reacts to current scenarios and cannot rely on upon taught or recalled data to make decisions in the present.

Limited Memory

It refers to an AI's ability to store previous data and predictions, using that data to make better predictions

AI as human

learns from human input and collaboration, focusing on algorithms that exist among a larger, human-based system
